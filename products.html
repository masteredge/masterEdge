PVC EDGE BANDING

Edge Banding Tapes are thin strips of plastic used to conceal the exposed edges of furniture. This increases the durability of edges and also gives them a beautiful appearance. These tapes are available in a wide variety of colours and wood grain designs due to which they can easily be matched with laminates or veneer. Edge banding is applied to the substrate by an automated process using a hot melt adhesive. This enables high volume manufacturing of furnitures. We offer a wide range of these wonder strips and are continuously working hard to introduce new designs and colours.
