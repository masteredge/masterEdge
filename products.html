PVC EDGE BANDING

Master Edge PVC EDGEBAND is used for concealing the edges of furniture & panels to give a clean solid wood appearance. & also makes the edges smoother to prevent any injury. The installation is also very simple which makes it popular among users. The designs and colours of the edge banding are very closely matched with all the popular colours of related products like laminates and pre-laminated boards. The R&D dept. is committed to introduce new colours and styles in every few days. We already have many shades to offer and many more to come. Every batch is tested by qualified technicians to ensure that only the best material is served to customers. Also, we are the first to introduce 50 meter packaging in Asia which already has a very good impact in every market and due to small packing size, it has reached to every segment.
